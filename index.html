About

01 / 05
Two decades on scaffolding and site floors — turning drawings into rooms people actually live and work in.

Premjit Maity has spent over 21 years as a Foreman across interior fit-out and construction projects in the Delhi NCR region, overseeing stone work, ceiling work, and full interior execution from groundwork to handover.

He has worked under two major contracting firms — Takshaka Interior Pvt. Ltd. (2005–2020) and Purshottam Contractor / KCS Construction Pvt. Ltd. (2020–2026) — leading site teams on premium hospitality, residential, and commercial builds including The Westin, JW Marriott, Godrej, and M3M developments.

His role combines hands-on technical supervision with project and money management — coordinating labour, materials, timelines, and budgets to keep large sites moving without compromising finish quality.
